About Enotria SA

I fell in love with wine as a teenager, inspired in part by Thomas Jefferson's passion for the same. "Good wine is a necessity of life for me," he wrote, and it became that for me at a very young age.

Through my years of undergrad and graduate studies I worked with numerous restaurants and caterers, affording me a pretty good wine education on a student budget. A stint at the renowned Inn at Little Washington restaurant in Virginia exposed me to the very finest that France and California had to offer.

A return to school at University of Virginia Law definitely engrained the importance of value, and my restaurant work those years included a deep dive into Italian wines that has helped me ever since . . .

Opting for wine over jurisprudence, after graduation I moved to San Antonio to run the wine department for Gabriel's Wine & Spirits. After 5 years I shifted to wholesale with Block Distributing (now Republic National Distributing Corporation).

I was subsequently hired by Don's & Ben's and managed their wine program from their Seazars store in Alamo Heights. When they were bought out by Gabriel's, I was moved out to their 22,000 square foot store at Blanco & 1604.

They let me go just before filing Chapter 11 in January of 2020, and I immediately began taking care of my clients through my friend's St. Trumyphon Winery in Sisterdale. Four years and a quarter million miles later I realized I was killing myself and finally got my own license and a space 5 minutes from my home in Alamo Heights.

I initially thought to open as a wine bar, but a good friend observed that — even with myself & I helping me — I would have no life! Making that component a members only club has afforded me the best of both worlds: my sales to the general public continue undisturbed by day, and my evenings and weekends feature a fun group of new friends sharing great wines in my cozy space. But without my ever (OK, rarely) having to stay into the wee hours!

I hope you'll soon reach out to me for your necessities of life!

Joe Baker

Wine Lover  ·  Enotria SA Founder
